# BusinessGPT Certifications

## Overview

Eraneos is committed to maintaining the highest standards of security and compliance for BusinessGPT. This page outlines the certifications and compliance frameworks that our platform adheres to, demonstrating our commitment to protecting your data and meeting regulatory requirements.

## ISO 27001

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/iso-27001.png" alt="ISO 27001 Certification" />
</Frame>

ISO 27001 is an internationally recognized standard for information security management systems (ISMS). Our certification demonstrates that BusinessGPT meets rigorous security requirements:

- Systematic approach to managing sensitive company information
- Comprehensive risk assessment and management
- Implementation of a coherent set of information security controls
- Continuous monitoring, review, and improvement of our security posture

## TISAX

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/tisax.png" alt="TISAX Assessment" />
</Frame>

TISAX (Trusted Information Security Assessment Exchange) is an assessment and exchange mechanism for information security in the automotive industry. Our TISAX assessment confirms:

- Compliance with VDA ISA (Information Security Assessment) requirements
- Protection of prototype parts and information
- Secure handling of data with protection needs
- Implementation of appropriate security controls for the automotive sector

## GDPR Compliance

BusinessGPT is designed and operated in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the European Union's comprehensive data protection law:

- Transparent data processing practices
- Implementation of data protection by design and default
- Support for data subject rights
- Comprehensive data processing agreements
- Regular data protection impact assessments

For detailed information about our GDPR compliance measures, please contact your account representative.

## SOC 2

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/soc2.png" alt="SOC 2 Compliance" />
</Frame>

SOC 2 (Service Organization Control 2) is a framework for managing customer data based on five "trust service principles":

- **Security**: Protection against unauthorized access
- **Availability**: System availability for operation and use
- **Processing Integrity**: System processing is complete, accurate, timely, and authorized
- **Confidentiality**: Information designated as confidential is protected
- **Privacy**: Personal information is collected, used, retained, and disclosed in conformity with commitments

BusinessGPT has successfully completed SOC 2 Type II audits, demonstrating our ongoing commitment to these principles.

## Certification Process

Our certification process includes:

1. **Preparation**: Implementing required controls and policies
2. **Gap Analysis**: Identifying and addressing any compliance gaps
3. **Pre-Assessment**: Internal audits to verify readiness
4. **Formal Assessment**: Evaluation by accredited third-party auditors
5. **Certification**: Formal recognition of compliance
6. **Continuous Monitoring**: Ongoing compliance verification
