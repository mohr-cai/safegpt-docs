# BusinessGPT Rollout Playbook

This playbook provides a detailed, actionable plan for rolling out BusinessGPT across your organization. It builds on the concepts introduced in the [Strategic GenAI Transformation](/admin/rollout-setup) guide and offers specific steps for each phase of implementation.

## Pre-rollout checklist

Before beginning your BusinessGPT rollout, ensure you have:

<CheckList>
  - Secured executive sponsorship and leadership buy-in
  - Identified an internal AI owner/champion
  - Defined clear objectives and success metrics
  - Assessed your organization's technical readiness
  - Reviewed security and compliance requirements
  - Allocated budget for training and support
</CheckList>

## Week-by-week implementation plan

### Week 1-2: Planning and preparation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Form your AI implementation team">
    Assemble a cross-functional team including representatives from IT, HR, legal, and key business units. Assign clear roles and responsibilities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define use cases">
    Identify 3-5 high-impact use cases that align with business objectives. Focus on areas where AI can solve existing pain points.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a communication plan">
    Develop messaging that explains the benefits of BusinessGPT, addresses concerns, and sets realistic expectations. Plan how and when to communicate with different stakeholder groups.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up technical infrastructure">
    Work with IT to ensure all technical prerequisites are met, including SSO configuration, network requirements, and security settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Week 3-4: Pilot program

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select pilot participants">
    Choose 15-30 users from different departments who are enthusiastic about new technology. Include both technical and non-technical users.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Conduct initial training">
    Provide comprehensive training sessions covering:
    - Basic functionality and navigation
    - Prompt engineering best practices
    - Use case examples relevant to their roles
    - Security and responsible use guidelines
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch pilot">
    Give participants access to BusinessGPT and assign specific tasks aligned with identified use cases. Schedule regular check-ins to provide support.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gather and analyze feedback">
    Collect feedback through surveys, interviews, and usage analytics. Identify what's working well and what needs improvement.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Week 5-8: Expanded deployment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refine approach based on pilot feedback">
    Update training materials, documentation, and implementation strategy based on lessons learned during the pilot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Develop internal resources">
    Create a knowledge base with:
    - Frequently asked questions
    - Best practices and prompt examples
    - Success stories from the pilot
    - Troubleshooting guides
  </Step>
  <Step title="Train department champions">
    Identify and train champions within each department who can provide peer support and promote adoption.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Roll out to additional departments">
    Expand access to BusinessGPT in phases, prioritizing departments based on use case alignment and readiness.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Week 9-12: Full organization rollout

<Steps>
  <Step title="Scale training program">
    Implement a comprehensive training program including:
    - Self-service learning resources
    - Department-specific workshops
    - Advanced sessions for power users
  </Step>
  <Step title="Establish support system">
    Create a tiered support structure:
    - Level 1: Department champions and self-help resources
    - Level 2: Internal AI team
    - Level 3: BusinessGPT technical support
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor adoption and impact">
    Track key metrics including:
    - Active users and engagement
    - Types of queries and use cases
    - Time savings and productivity improvements
    - User satisfaction
  </Step>
  <Step title="Celebrate and share successes">
    Recognize early adopters and showcase success stories through internal communications, highlighting tangible benefits achieved.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Ongoing management

After the initial rollout, focus on these areas to ensure continued success:

### Continuous improvement

- Regularly gather user feedback through surveys and focus groups
- Monitor usage patterns to identify opportunities for improvement
- Stay updated on new BusinessGPT features and capabilities
- Refine prompts and workflows based on performance data

### Knowledge sharing

- Host regular community of practice meetings for users to share tips and use cases
- Maintain an internal repository of effective prompts and templates
- Create case studies highlighting successful implementations
- Facilitate cross-department collaboration on AI initiatives

### Governance and compliance

- Regularly review usage patterns for compliance with policies
- Update guidelines as needed based on emerging best practices
- Conduct periodic security reviews
- Ensure all users complete refresher training annually
