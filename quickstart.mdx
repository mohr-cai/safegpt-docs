## Setting Up Your Account

Follow these steps to set up your BusinessGPT account and start using the platform.

### Account Creation and Setup

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="Create your account">
    1. Visit your dedicated BusinessGPT domain (provided by your administrator)
    2. Enter your business email and create a password
    3. Verify your email address through the confirmation link
    4. Complete your profile with your name and organization details
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Set up your organization">
    1. Create or join an organization
    2. Invite team members by entering their email addresses
    3. Assign appropriate roles and permissions
    4. Configure organization settings according to your needs
    <Note>
      Enterprise customers can integrate with their existing identity provider (IdP) for single sign-on (SSO) capabilities. Contact your account manager for more information.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Initial Configuration

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="gears" title="Configure your workspace">
    1. Navigate to Settings > Workspace
    2. Set up your default language and timezone
    3. Configure data retention policies
    4. Set up custom branding (Enterprise plan only)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="folder-open" title="Set up your projects">
    BusinessGPT allows you to create projects for organizing document collections:

    1. Go to the Projects section in the side menu
    2. Click the "+" symbol to create a new project
    3. Give your project a descriptive name and optional description
    4. Upload relevant documents to your project
    5. Share the project with team members if needed
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
